Parth OS● shipped & live

A personal AI chief‑of‑staff: a Telegram bot (text + voice) and a dashboard sharing one persistent memory — tasks, notes, facts, expenses, habits, a 7 a.m. briefing. MVP shipped in a day, compounding since.

next.js · supabase · groq · whisper → github.com/tiniparth/parthOS

open the file

The itch: every assistant forgets. Tasks in one app, expenses in another, thoughts nowhere. I wanted one door — message anything, in any shape, and have it land in the right place and be remembered.

  • One door: a Telegram bot that takes text or a rambling voice note in any phrasing — “spent 200 on lunch”, “call the plumber Friday” — and routes it: task, note, expense, event, fact, or just conversation.
  • One memory: facts, people, goals, clients, habits, journal — all in Postgres, loaded into every AI call. The assistant actually knows its user.
  • It runs my mornings: a 7 a.m. briefing (weather → calendar → tasks → the one thing that matters most), an evening nudge, and a weekly “week in review.”
  • It writes my record: mention a real win in passing and it quietly logs it to an append‑only milestone file — a self‑writing appraisal document.

Under the hood: Next.js 15 on Vercel · Supabase Postgres · Groq LLMs · Whisper for voice · cron for the rituals. Single‑user by design.
